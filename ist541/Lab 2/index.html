How to Wash Your Hands

By Nichol Veles


Step 1: Wet the hands

Make sure you have the water at a comfortable degree so that you do not burn yourself. Get your hands completely wet using clean, running water, either warm or cold, and apply soap.

Step 2: Lather

Scrub every part of your hands by rubbing them together with the soap. Lather the backs of the hands, between the fingers, and under the nails.

Step 3: Scrub

You can count to 20 or sing the Happy Birthday song to yourself and that shouldl conclude the 20 seconds needed to get every part of your hands.

Step 4: Rinse

Make sure to rinse off all soap. You may optionally place lotion or other products on the skin.

Step 5: Dry

Air dry is preferred , but times do not always allow you the time to do so. Make sure you use a clean dry towel or paper towel to dry your hands.
